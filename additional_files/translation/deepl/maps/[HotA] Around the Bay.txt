===== Kartenname =====
Around the Bay

===== Kartenbeschreibung =====
You will face a classic battle against three active neighbours on this scenic horseshoe-like island.

===== Getimete Ereignisse =====

Name:
Day 1
Nachricht:
You welcome the dawn standing on a balcony oh the highest tower of your castle. For some time you admire the views opening to you from the height: mountain peaks, tips of spruces, silhouettes of far-away forts. All of that drowns in a dense morning fog. Your opponents hide somewhere in this silvery darkness. A new day begins, and you inhale the fresh air with a noise. Today is a day of a good fight!
